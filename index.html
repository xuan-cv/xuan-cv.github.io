Shuxing Xuan

I am currently a fourth-year PhD candidate at the School of Automation, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, and am preparing to commence my postdoctoral research. I received the B.E. degree in Automation in 2019 and the M.E. degree in Control Theory and Control Engineering in 2022 from Lanzhou University of Technology. During my academic training, I received multiple awards in mathematical modeling competitions and was honored with the National Scholarship for Doctoral Students. My future research will further focus on the modeling, learning, and control of swarm intelligence systems and their applications in complex engineering systems.

Research

My early research interests focused on deep learning and image processing, while my current research is centered on cooperative control of multi-agent systems and the extension of reinforcement learning methodologies, with particular emphasis on distributed control, optimal decision-making, safety-constrained control, and the theoretical analysis and engineering applications of swarm intelligence systems.
