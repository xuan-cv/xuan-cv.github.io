Shuxing Xuan received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in automation and control theory and control engineering from the Lanzhou University of Technology,
Lanzhou, China, in 2019 and 2022. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from University of Electronic Science
and Technology of China, Chengdu, China. His current research interests include multiagent cooperative control and reinforcement learning.
